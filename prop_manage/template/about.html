About PropManager

Empowering Kenyans with safe, transparent, and efficient property management solutions.

Born from the frustration of house hunting scams and unreliable property management, our team of three passionate developers decided to create a platform that puts tenants and landlords first.

Our Mission

To revolutionize property management in Kenya by providing a transparent, secure, and user-friendly platform that connects reliable landlords with quality tenants, eliminating the stress and uncertainty of traditional house hunting.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's leading property management platform, setting the standard for transparency, reliability, and customer satisfaction in the real estate industry while contributing to the growth of safe and affordable housing solutions.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Trust & Transparency

We believe in complete transparency in all our dealings. No hidden fees, no surprises, just honest and reliable service.

Customer First

Every decision we make is centered around providing the best experience for our users. Your success is our success.

Innovation

We continuously innovate to solve real problems faced by Kenyans in the property market, making processes simpler and more efficient.

Our Story

It all started with a simple frustration - the endless cycle of house hunting scams and unreliable property management in Kenya. Three friends, all tech enthusiasts, decided enough was enough.

After experiencing firsthand the challenges of finding decent accommodation in Nairobi, we came together to build a solution that would make property management transparent, reliable, and user-friendly.

What began as a side project has grown into a platform that's helping thousands of Kenyans find their perfect homes without the stress and uncertainty of traditional house hunting.
